Brief history of computers

by Sudeshna Das

Computers are an essential part of our lives. These machines, which can perform complex operations in a very short time, are a blessing of modern science. Or are they? A Brief History of Computers will demystify computers for you by giving you the story of how computers began long before we think they did and where they are headed next.
